About Faze Clan

Faze Clan is an American esports and entertainment organization headquartered in Los Angeles. It was founded in 2010 and has since grown to become one of the most prominent esports teams globally.

We compete in various popular video games such as Call of Duty, Counter-Strike: Global Offensive, Fortnite, FIFA, and more. Our players and content creators have millions of followers across social media platforms.
